Student Quick Start

Get up and running with QA Pilot Academy in minutes. This guide walks you through your first login, course enrollment, and daily workflow.

Getting Started

1
Log In to Your Account

Open the portal and sign in with the email and password provided by your training coordinator. If you're exploring, use the "Try Demo Account" button to access a pre-configured demo.

2
Browse Available Courses

Once logged in, you'll see the "Available Courses" section. Courses are organized by category — QA Fundamentals, Development, and Capstone assessments. Each card shows the course title, module count, and estimated time.

3
Enroll in a Course

Click any course card to enroll. You'll be taken directly into the course viewer where you can start learning immediately. Your progress is saved automatically as you complete modules.

💡 Tip — Course Prerequisites Some courses require completion of a prerequisite before you can enroll. For example, Capstone 1 requires QA Onboarding Training. Completed prerequisites are indicated with a green checkmark in your sidebar.

Navigating a Course

Each course is divided into modules, and each module contains sub-modules (lessons, labs, and quizzes). Use the sidebar on the left to jump between modules or click "Next" at the bottom of each lesson.

Lesson Types

  • Reading lessons — Text-based content with examples. Click "Mark Complete" when you're done.
  • Interactive Labs — Hands-on simulations of Dynamics 365 CRM or Azure DevOps. Follow the guided steps.
  • Quizzes — Multiple-choice questions to test your understanding. Quizzes are learning-focused — no pass/fail gate.
  • Capstone Assessments — Real-world scenarios that test your cumulative skills. These are evaluated against a rubric.

Progress Tracking

Your progress is shown as a percentage bar on each enrolled course card. Completed modules are marked in the sidebar, and your overall course progress updates in real time. You can resume any course from where you left off.

Certificates & Completion

When you complete all modules in a course, you'll earn a certificate. Here's what to expect:

✓
Certificate Awarded

Upon completing the final module, a certificate is automatically generated. You'll see a confetti animation and be redirected to your certificate page.

✓
View Your Certificates

All earned certificates are listed in the sidebar under "Certificates". Click any certificate to view or print it. Certificates include your name, course title, completion date, and a unique credential ID.

✓
Next Course Auto-Enrollment

When you complete a course that unlocks a follow-up, you'll be automatically enrolled in the next course. For example, completing QA Onboarding Training unlocks Capstone Assessment 1.

📄 Printing Your Certificate Certificates are designed for printing. Use your browser's Print command (⌘P) to save as PDF or print a physical copy. Each certificate includes a double-border credential-style layout.

Getting Help

If you get stuck or have questions:

  • Your Training Coordinator — Reach out to your instructor for course guidance, password resets, or enrollment questions.
  • Facilitator Guide — Your training coordinator has a detailed facilitator guide with platform administration instructions.
  • Data Privacy — All your learning data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

For platform issues, contact your training coordinator directly.
