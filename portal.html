Let's get you oriented in 3 quick steps.

Welcome to QA Pilot Academy — your interactive QA training platform.

📚
Structured Courses

Work through modules with reading, exercises, and quizzes.

🖥️
Real Simulated Tools

Practice in realistic Dynamics CRM, ADO, Teams, and Outlook simulators.

🏆
Certificates

Earn a certificate for each completed course with your name and score.

📤
Report Progress

Send your results to your instructor with one click.

Here's how your training works:

  1. Browse courses in the catalog below — each is made up of modules with lessons and quizzes.
  2. Click a course to open it. Work through each module step by step. Your progress saves automatically.
  3. Complete all modules and pass the quizzes to earn your certificate with your name and score.
  4. Some courses include a capstone assessment — a hands-on scenario in a simulated Windows desktop environment.

Your progress is saved locally in your browser. You can close the page and come back anytime.

When you're done with your training, here's how to share your results:

  1. Complete your courses — work through all modules and quizzes at your own pace.
  2. Click Download Student Data in the top bar — a file with your progress will download.
  3. If your instructor's email is configured, your Outlook will open with the file ready to attach.
  4. Send the file to your instructor. They can import your results into their dashboard.
💡 Tip: You can re-open this walkthrough anytime from the user menu (click your avatar in the top-right corner).
